A Tribute to my Mama

In high school, our English teacher tasked us with writing a Haiku poem about the person we love the most. I chose to write my haiku about my mother who passed away just a week before this assignment was given.

A: The one I loved the most is my mama.

B: Her passing has brought me closer for sure.

A: When I was born all I said was gaga.

B: But she understood me; her love was pure.


C: When I was five I won the Angels Crown.

D: From her I get my sweet voice and bright smile.

C: She taught me to look up and never down.

D: Even when her health presented a trial.


E: She was there to see me get my letter.

F: At her service we said a fond goodbye.

E: I miss her, but I know she's all better.

F: I love her, and think of her and sigh.


G: Her sweet pure name is Teethina Cherry

G: I want to be like her when I marry.

Forever my Mama
